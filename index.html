Information

This style is written only to work with ccd'0s 4chanX fork.

This style currently only works on firefox.

Big thanks to mudanaku for updating and fixing this style.

To get the board links to display properly, use the custom navigation found in the advanced section of the settings. Click here for an example.

Use SHIFT + H to goggle the sidebar.

Click "掲示板" to open the full board list.

Click "見てのスレッド" to open the thread watcher menu.

Press 'Q' to open quick reply.

A modern look for your chinese cartoon discussion boards

Installation

Install ccd0's 4chanX userscript.

Install the Stylish extension.

Navigate to the userstyles section of the Addons page.

Click on "Write New Style", give it a name, and paste the CSS there.

Go into your 4chan X settings.

Turn OFF 'Persistent QR', I'm not sure if there are any other settings needed for it to look right, so my settings can be found here.

Customization

CTRL + F 'http://i.imgur.com/2Uw0eQQ.png' and replace it with a path to your own 1920x64 header image.

CTRL + F '#3F51B5' and replace it with a hexdecimal color of your choice to change the accent color.
